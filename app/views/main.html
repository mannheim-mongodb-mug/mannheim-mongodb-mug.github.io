What is MongoDB?

MongoDB ("humongous") is a free, high-performance open-source, cross-platform, schema less database (noSQL) storing JSON-like documents.

Our Goals?

Exchange of ideas and expertise, help new users. Build up and curate a strong, open mongoDB user community in the Rhine-Neckar area. Maybe world domination.
